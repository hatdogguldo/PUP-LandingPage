Welcome to the PUP SANTA ROSA Branch Virtual Tour!

This tour will give you a brief overview of the different facilities on campus, including Admission and Registration Services, Student Records Section, Administrative Services Office, Student Affairs and Services Office, Cashier's Office, Director's Office, Conference Room, Faculty Lounge, Lecture Room, Computer Laboratory, Chemistry Laboratory, Physics Laboratory, Industrial Engineering Laboratory, Electronics Engineering Laboratory, ICT Services, Library, Clinic, Student Lounge/Snack Center, and Gymnasium.

We will start with Admission and Registration Services, where you can learn about the different programs offered at PUP SANTA ROSA and how to apply. Next, we will visit the Student Records Section, where you can get copies of your transcripts and other academic records. After that, we will take a look at the Administrative Services Office, which handles a variety of tasks such as financial aid, campus safety, and housing.

We will then move on to the Student Affairs and Services Office, where you can find help with everything from academic advising to career planning. Next, we will visit the Cashier's Office, where you can pay your tuition and fees. After that, we will take a look at the Director's Office, which is the central hub of campus administration.

Finally, we will visit the different facilities that students use on a daily basis, including the Conference Room, Faculty Lounge, Lecture Room, Computer Laboratory, Chemistry Laboratory, Physics Laboratory, Industrial Engineering Laboratory, Electronics Engineering Laboratory, ICT Services, Library, Clinic, Student Lounge/Snack Center, and Gymnasium.
